WHAT IS AV & AV TECHNOLOGY?

AV is an abbreviation for Audio Visual, which is used to describe electronic media that possess an
audio (sound) and visual (sight) component. AV technology refers to the actual equipment or tools
that convey these components, such as lighting, projectors, speakers, and displays.
